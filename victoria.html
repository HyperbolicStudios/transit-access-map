Victoria Vancouver

Travel time to UVic by Transit

All time estimates are based on calculating a trip that would arrive at the chosen destination at 8:30AM on a September weekday. The start point of each trip is the centroid of the census DA. Data sources are the Google Maps API and BC Transit's Open Data.

A travel time estimate of -1 indicates that that mode of travel is not available for that particular location. Do not rely on this for trip planning purposes; I make no representations about the accuracy of this data.

This project is open sourced on Github.
